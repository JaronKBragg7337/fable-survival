# VISION.md — What This Game Is Trying to Become

The goal is NOT to build "another survival game" or to chase feature count.

The goal is a browser-based, DayZ-style survival sandbox that:

1. **Loads instantly on a phone from a link.** No install, ever. A kid can text
   the link to a friend and they're playing in ten seconds.
2. **Is maintained by AI sessions over weeks and months.** The repository is the
   memory. Any session, any model, can pick up and continue.
3. **Grows continuously without ever breaking.** The live link always works.
   Progress is additive; the foundation is protected.
4. **Feels alive**: danger at night, a safe place to return to, reasons to go out
   (loot, resources), reasons to come back (trader, base), and visible progress
   (your base, your repaired car, your gear).

**Who it's for:** the owner's son and his friends, playing on phones. Fun,
readable, and fast beats realistic. If a change looks amazing but drops phone
FPS or confuses a 10-year-old, it's wrong.

**Every change should move toward this vision.** If a proposed feature doesn't
serve points 1–4, it goes to the Icebox, not the codebase.
